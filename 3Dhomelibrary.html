Important Notice: Metropolitan Trust Bank Closure

Dear Valued Customer,

It is with profound regret that we announce the closure of Metropolitan Trust Bank, effective June 11, 2025. Following our declaration of bankruptcy on June 5, 2025, federal regulators have appointed the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) as receiver.

After 35 years of service, economic challenges and unprecedented market conditions have made it impossible for us to continue operations. This decision was not made lightly and comes after exhaustive efforts to secure the bank's future.
